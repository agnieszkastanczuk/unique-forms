about

Founded in 2020, Unique Forms is a ceramics company that prides itself on its hand-crafted, artistic pieces. With a focus on quality and attention to detail, each piece is carefully crafted by our team of skilled artisans. The company was started by Leia Kowalsky, a graduate of the Academy of Fine Arts, who brings her passion for ceramics and creativity to every product. All of our ceramics are made in Poland, making us a truly homegrown company.

"Ceramics is the art of creating beauty out of clay. It allows us to mold and shape the world around us, bringing function and form together in a way that is both practical and aesthetically pleasing. We hope to bring joy and beauty to homes around the world with our one-of-a-kind ceramics."
- Leia Kowalsky
